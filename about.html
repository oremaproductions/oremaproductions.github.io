About Orema Productions

Orema Productions is more than a photography brand — it’s a living legacy. Founded by Cidney Jones in January 2021, Orema (affectionately known as OP) began as a passion project and quickly grew into a timeless expression of storytelling through imagery.

Cidney picked up her first camera at 18 and has since captured the stories of hundreds across Illinois. While pursuing her undergraduate degree at the University of Illinois, OP became more than a name — it became a presence. Known for its raw emotion, intentional artistry, and open- minded approach, OP earned recognition not just for its quality, but for the soul behind every frame.

At its heart, Orema Productions exists to leave a lasting artistic imprint on everyone it touches. Each image invites you to pause, reflect, and feel — offering not just visuals, but connection. OP isn’t exclusive in the traditional sense. Instead, it draws in those who crave depth, resonance, and authenticity in art.

The name Orema is a tribute to Cidney’s late father, an artist whose brilliance never had the chance to be fully seen. Orema is his first name — Amero — spelled backwards. Through this homage, Cidney carries his spirit forward, transforming what he left behind into something unforgettable.

In 2024, OP introduced the WareHouse — a creative model collaboration program designed for artists and models to come together on passion-driven projects. It’s a space where vision meets purpose, and portfolios grow in bold, meaningful ways.

OP is built on selfless passion, relentless work, strategic vision, and a deep commitment to storytelling.

Welcome to Orema Productions — where every frame is a feeling, and every image is a piece of legacy.

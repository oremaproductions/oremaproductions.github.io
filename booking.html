2. How We Use Your Information

  • To communicate with you about our photography services
  • To process payments and send receipts

We implement security measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information. However, we may share your information with trusted third-party service providers (payment processors, marketing services) to assist with business operations.

5. Your Rights

7. Third-Party Links

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated date will be reflected at the top.

2. Booking & Payment

  • A non-refundable 50% deposit is required to secure your booking, unless otherwise specified. The deposit is deducted from the total cost of the session.
